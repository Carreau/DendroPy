shallow-copy
    All member objects are references, except for :attr:`annotation_set` of
    top-level object and member :class:`Annotation` objects: these are full,
    independent instances (though any complex objects in the `value` field of
    :class:`Annotation` objects are also just references).

"taxon-namespace-scoped copy"
    All member objects are full independent instances, *except* for
    :class:`TaxonNamespace` and :class:`Taxon` instances: these are references.

"total copy"
    Exhaustive deep-copy: all objects are cloned.
